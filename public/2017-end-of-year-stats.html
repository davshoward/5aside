2017 End of year Stats

  • This year saw the first match played away from Withdean, at Manor Road in Whitehawk.
  • 24 players played in total in 2017, an increase over 2015 and 2016's total of 18.
  • Sam retained 1st place from last year, with 70pts - a lead of 10 - but just 1 more than last year's total.
  • Strangely, Sam finished with the exact same goal difference as last year: 56.
  • Rob notched a second 3rd place finish, whilst Dan improved from 4th last year to 2nd in 2017.
  • Andy took the top spot for attendance, with an 89% attendance record (not enough to beat last year's impressive 95% attendance from Chris and Davs).
  • The points per game league was narrowly won by Sam (P39), on 1.79 PPG. Kris (P21) and Mike (P17) sit 0.03 PPG behind in joint second place. Alex (P10) took 4th place and Dan (P38) took 5th with 1.58.
  • Chris had the biggest consecutive streak of the year, beginning at the start of the year and finishing on 12th June - a total of 17 appearences.
  • This year saw a marginal decrease in goal difference per game, at 3.44, down from 3.67 in 2016.
  • 2017 saw a decreased proportion of handicap victories - 39%, down 6% on last year. There were 3 draws this year though, compared to none in 2016.
  • 9 new players played in 2017, compared to 3 in 2016.
  • This year saw 139 more goals than last year.
  • 7 members joined the century club - Chris, Davs, Sam, Andy, Steve, Rob and Dan. It'll take the best part of a year before we'll likely see another member.
  • Chris increased his lead at the top of the all time attendance list to 10 games, with a total of 118 appearances.
  • Roscoe moved into the top 10 attendance rankings with 49 in total, with a 70% attendance rate since his debut last year.
  • Sam has now taken top spot from Davs in the overall rankings and holds a 10 point lead.
